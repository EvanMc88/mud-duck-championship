About The Event

What started years ago as a humble gathering of a single 4-some has evolved into a full-blown weekend of golf, drinks, and duck-related shenanigans. Over the last six years, the Mud Duck Championship has become the fall highlight for friends who love competition, camaraderie, and the occasional muddy shoe.

Each year, the victorious team takes home the coveted Mud Duck Trophy, while the spirit of chaos is celebrated by awarding the infamous Muddiest Duck Trophy to the player who embodies everything this weekend stands for: poor shots, great stories, and an unparalleled ability to embrace the mud, the laughter, and maybe a drink or two.
