About The Event

What started years ago as a humble 4-some has evolved into a weekend of golf, drinks, and duck-related shenanigans. Over six years, the Mud Duck Championship has become the fall highlight for friends who love competition and camaraderie.

Each year, the victorious team takes home the Mud Duck Trophy, while the spirit of chaos is celebrated with the infamous Muddiest Duck Trophy for the duck who embodies the mud, the laughter, and maybe a drink or two.
