The Event

The Mud Duck Championship is a legendary fall weekend where 28 Ducks battle over 36 holes. There will be mud. There will be drinks. There will be stories that grow taller every year. Prestige optional. Traditions mandatory.

About The Championship

Established in 2020, The Mud Duck Championship brings together competitors for team glory, individual recognition, and questionable decision-making.
