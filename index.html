The Event

The Mud Duck Championship is a legendary fall weekend where 28 Ducks battle over 36 holes. Mud, drinks, and chaos are mandatory. Stories get taller every year; glory is optional.

About The Championship

What started many years ago as a small thing has blown up in the past 6 years into what it is now: a 28 person, Ryder Cup-style event of some of the finest alcohol-consuming / pretenders of golfers around. Together, these Ducks make one heck of a weekend group.

For one weekend every fall, the Ducks put aside jobs, families, friendships, and respect for their own bodies to battle it out for the coveted Mud Duck Championship. It has become a fantastic way to stay connected over the years by using golf as a disguise to hide our love of a 48-hour party. Mud, mischief, and questionable swings are all part of the fun.

Each year, teams clash, trophies are awarded, and the Muddiest Duck emerges as the champion of chaos and revelry. It's serious competition, but don’t let that fool you – the real goal is friendship, laughs, and maybe surviving with dignity (mostly). 🦆🍺
