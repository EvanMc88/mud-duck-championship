The Mud Duck Championship

28 Ducks • 36 Holes • Chaos & Drinks

The Event

The Mud Duck Championship is a legendary fall weekend where 28 Ducks battle over 36 holes. Mud, drinks, and chaos are mandatory. Stories get taller every year; glory is optional.

About The Championship

Established in 2020, The Mud Duck Championship brings together competitors for team glory, individual recognition, and questionable decision-making. Ducks and beers collide in perfect chaos!
